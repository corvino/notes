** Strengths & Weaknesses
If a Dragon is strong aginst another, they will deal 20% more DMG. If a
Dragon is weak against another, they will deal 20% less DMG.
** Elemental Resonance
If your team is made up of Dragons of the same Type the team will get a
stat bonus. (Light, Dark, and Chaos Dragons will not count.)
