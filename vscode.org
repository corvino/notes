** Snippets
*** Attach to chrome/web
{
  "name": "Renderer",
  "type": "chrome",
  "request": "attach",
  "port": 9223,
  "webRoot": "${workspaceFolder}",
  "timeout": 5000
}
*** Front-end tooling (webpack, vite, etc)
{
  "name": "react",
  "command": "npm start",
  "request": "launch",
  "type": "node-terminal"
}
