*** Installing [[https://github.com/ungoogled-software/ungoogled-chromium?tab=readme-ov-file#downloads][ungoogled-chromium]]

Available on macOS through Homebrew.

#+begin_src sh
brew install --cask ungoogled-chromium
#+end_src

To see info on how ungoogled-chromium differs from Chromium open
[[chrome://ungoogled-first-run]] in ungoogled-chromium. This includes
information on [[https://github.com/NeverDecaf/chromium-web-store][extensions]] and extra features.
